The Daily Life of a Vampire

Being a Vampire is way more difficult than it needs to be. I can't be in the sun for obvious, I will die reasons. So I sleep all day and can only go out at night. Speaking of which, do you have any idea how hard it is to get a non-Dunkin Donuts breakfast sandwich at 9PM? Difficult! I also live in a basement, because I can't have any windows in my place without risking dying from slight sun exposure.

Those Twilight vampires really had it so easy. Oh no! We get all shiny in the sun, how traumatizing! I wish! I guess one good thing that came out of Twilight is a lot of people are really into vampires now, so dating isn't too bad. Except I can't be out too long, or I might try to suck my date's blood, which that probably wouldn't leave a good impression. Probably.

Turning into a bat can be pretty cool though, even if a little unsafe at times. Most people don't like having bats in their houses. And since I can only work night shifts I work at a 24-hour 7-eleven, which is gets very boring at most hours. Better than working at a cemetery though like you'd think I would, those are creepy.

Maybe I should move to London, they don't seem to get much sun at all in the UK.

From,

Your friendly Neighborhood Vampire
