My Biography

My name is Michael Reilly and I am a 3rd year Computer Science student here at Stevens. I am from Rockaway, NJ, about a 1 hour and 15 minute train ride from Hoboken. A fun fact about me is that I am a triplet. My sisters names are Emily and Nicole. I also am an activist, particularly LGBTQ+ issues, as I am gay myself.

Some jobs I have had include being a cashier at JCPenneys, being a a subcontractor for a medical company, and currently I work in registration at a COVID-19 vaccination megasite. Working as a cashier and needing to clean changing rooms taught me how gross people can be, even in public. Working as a subcontractor was the only job I've ever had where I used my Computer Science knowledge and skills. And I think most people would be surprised how irritable people can be while needing to register for getting the COVID-19 vaccine, even knowing they will definitely be getting it that day.
